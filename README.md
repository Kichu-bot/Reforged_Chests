Reforged Chests

Reforged Chests is an advanced storage mod for Minecraft that introduces a comprehensive, tiered chest system. Designed for both vanilla survival purists and modded power-users, this mod allows you to scale your storage from humble wooden chests to high-capacity, reinforced Netherite-tier structures.

>Key Features (Beta v0.1)

55 Chest Variants: A complete collection covering 11 wood types across 5 progression tiers.

Smithing Table Integration: Upgrade your diamond-tier chests to Netherite using the Smithing Table, fitting perfectly into Minecraft's late-game progression.

High-Fidelity Animations: Custom 3D models powered by GeckoLib, featuring fluid opening and closing animations.

Automation Ready: Fully compatible with hoppers and other automated item transport systems.

Balanced Progression: Higher-tier chests (T4 and T5) require Diamond+ level pickaxes to break, protecting your hard-earned items in multiplayer environments.

>Roadmap to v1.0

This mod is currently in BETA. We are actively developing the following core features for the full release:

Modular Addon System: Expand your chests beyond just slot capacity. We are working on diverse functional addons to customize your storage experience.

In-World Upgrades: Upgrade your placed chests instantly using specific upgrade items without having to break the block or lose your items.

And more...
